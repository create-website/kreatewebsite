World Bank Open Data

Comprehensive collection of global development data, including indicators on finance, health, environment, and poverty.

United Nations (UNdata)

A vast repository of statistics from various UN agencies covering economic, social, financial, and industrial topics for countries worldwide.

The International Monetary Fund provides data on international finance, debt, exchange rates, and other economic and financial indicators.

A platform for data scientists with a huge collection of user-contributed datasets on a wide variety of topics, from finance to pop culture.

Google Dataset Search

A search engine from Google that helps researchers locate online data that is freely available for use across thousands of repositories.

OECD Data

The Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development provides comparable statistics on a wide range of subjects for its member countries.

Key Data: Economic, Development, Trade Visit Source →

Data.gov

The home of the U.S. Government's open data. You can find data, tools, and resources from federal, state, and local government agencies.

U.S. Census Bureau

The primary source for quality data about the nation's people and economy. Offers detailed demographic and economic data.

Key Data: Demographics, Social, Economic Visit Source →

Bureau of Labor Statistics

The principal federal agency responsible for measuring labor market activity, working conditions, and price changes in the economy.

Key Data: Economic, Employment, Labor Visit Source →

CDC WONDER

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention's Wide-ranging Online Data for Epidemiologic Research provides access to public health data.

Data.gov.in

India's open government data portal. It provides a single point of access to datasets published by various ministries and departments of the Indian government.

Key Data: Government, Social, Economic Visit Source →

Reserve Bank of India (DBIE)

The Database on Indian Economy is the central bank's primary data warehouse, offering time-series data on finance, banking, and the broader economy.

Key Data: Finance, Economic, Banking Visit Source →

National Data & Analytics Platform

An initiative by NITI Aayog, NDAP provides standardized datasets from various government sources, focusing on usability and accessibility.
