The Spirit of the Horse

The Chinese New Year in 2026 begins on **February 17th**, ushering in the Year of the Fire Horse. The Horse is the seventh animal in the zodiac cycle, known for its boundless energy, intelligence, and enthusiasm. People born in the Year of the Horse are considered active, sociable, and cheerful. The Fire element adds passion, creativity, and leadership to these traits, making 2026 a year predicted to be full of action, innovation, and adventure.
