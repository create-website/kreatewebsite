Happy Diwali 2025 Wishes in Rajasthani

Rajasthani Diwali wishes with English translations

1. दीपावली री घणी-घणी शुभकामना!

Many many good wishes for Diwali!

2. ई दिवाळी तोहार, आपरा जीवा म रोशनी भरि दे।

May this Diwali fill your life with light.

3. लक्ष्मीजी रो आशीर्वाद सदा आपरा संग रहो।

May Goddess Lakshmi’s blessings always stay with you.
