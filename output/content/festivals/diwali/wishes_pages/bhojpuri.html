Happy Diwali 2025 Wishes in Bhojpuri

Bhojpuri Diwali greetings with English translations

1. दीपावली के ढेर सारा शुभकामना!

Wishing you lots of Diwali happiness!

2. ई दिवाली रौशनी अउर खुशहाली लेके आवे।

May this Diwali bring light and prosperity.

3. लक्ष्मी माता के कृपा हमेशा तोहरा पर रहे।

May Goddess Lakshmi always shower her blessings on you.
