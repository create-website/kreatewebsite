The Story of Victory

Dussehra commemorates the epic victory of Lord Rama over the ten-headed demon king, Ravana, who had abducted Rama's wife, Sita. It is a festival that symbolizes the ultimate triumph of 'Dharma' (righteousness) over 'Adharma' (evil).

On this day, people celebrate new beginnings, seek blessings for their ventures, and burn effigies of Ravana to signify the destruction of evil within and without. It is a time for family, feasts, and festive joy across India.
