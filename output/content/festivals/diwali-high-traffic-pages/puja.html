Diwali Puja Vidhi & Muhurat 2025

Step-by-step guide and timings for Lakshmi Puja in 2025.

🪔 Puja Muhurat (21st October 2025)

  • Lakshmi Puja Muhurat: 05:45 PM – 07:45 PM
  • Pradosh Kaal: 05:45 PM – 08:15 PM
  • Vrishabha Kaal: 05:45 PM – 07:40 PM

🕉️ Puja Vidhi (Rituals)

  1. Clean and decorate the home with rangoli and diyas.
  2. Place idols of Goddess Lakshmi and Lord Ganesha on a decorated altar.
  3. Offer flowers, sweets, fruits, and coins during the puja.
  4. Chant mantras and perform aarti with family.
