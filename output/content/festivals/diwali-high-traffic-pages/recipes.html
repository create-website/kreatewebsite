Diwali Recipes

Learn how to make traditional Diwali sweets and snacks at home.

  • Laddoo: Sweet round balls made from flour, ghee, and sugar.
  • Kaju Katli: Cashew fudge cut into diamond shapes.
  • Chakli: A crispy spiral snack made from rice flour and spices.
  • Jalebi: Deep-fried spirals soaked in sugar syrup.
