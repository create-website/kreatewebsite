What is A/B Testing?

A/B testing (or split testing) is a method of comparing two versions of a webpage, app screen, or email against each other to determine which one performs better. You show the two variants to users at random and statistical analysis determines which variation is more effective for a given goal.

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

①

Ending Tests Too Early

Wait for a large enough sample size to ensure results are statistically significant.

②

Testing Too Many Things

Isolate changes to one element per test to know what caused the impact.

③

Ignoring External Factors

Be aware of holidays, marketing campaigns, or site changes that can skew data.
