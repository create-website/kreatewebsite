A Journey Through Time

Key milestones that paved the way for the modern AI revolution.

1950 - The Turing Test

Alan Turing proposes a test for machine intelligence, laying the philosophical groundwork for AI.

1997 - Deep Blue vs. Kasparov

IBM's Deep Blue chess computer defeats world champion Garry Kasparov, a landmark moment for AI in strategic competition.

2011 - Watson Wins Jeopardy!

IBM's Watson demonstrates mastery of natural language processing and knowledge retrieval on a popular quiz show.

2016 - AlphaGo's Victory

Google DeepMind's AlphaGo defeats Go champion Lee Sedol, showcasing the power of deep reinforcement learning.

2022 - Generative AI Goes Mainstream

Advanced models like GPT-3 and DALL-E 2 become widely accessible, enabling creation of human-like text and images.

What is Modern AI?

Today's AI is not a single entity, but a collection of powerful disciplines. Machine Learning forms the backbone, enabling systems to learn from data. Natural Language Processing allows them to understand and generate human language, while Computer Vision gives them the ability to "see" and interpret the visual world.
