Evolution of Machine Translation

The journey from rigid, rule-based systems to context-aware neural networks mirrors the advancement of AI itself.

1

Rule-Based (RBMT)

Relied on handcrafted grammatical rules and dictionaries. Laborious and inflexible.

2

Statistical (SMT)

Used statistical models learned from large volumes of human-translated text.

3

Neural (NMT)

Current state-of-the-art using deep neural networks to capture full sentence context.
