The Spectrum of Autonomy

Agency is not a binary switch. It's a journey of increasing capability and trust, from simple assistance to full autonomy. Each level represents a deliberate business decision to cede more control for greater efficiency and scale.

L1: Assisted Intelligence (Copilot)

System provides on-demand support for single-step tasks. User is in full control.

L2: Partial Automation (Workflow)

Executes predefined workflows but requires human approval at key checkpoints.

L3: Conditional Autonomy (Actor)

Independently plans and executes tasks, escalating to a human for edge cases.

L4: High Autonomy (Specialist)

Operates fully autonomously within its domain, handling failures without human intervention.

L5: Full Autonomy (Collaborative)

Operates without domain limitations, learning and adapting at a human-expert level.
