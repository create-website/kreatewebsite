Calculating Return on Investment (ROI)

While KPIs track operational performance, ROI translates this into financial terms. A holistic ROI calculation must account for both tangible benefits like cost savings and intangible value like improved decision quality.

Successful adoption is less a technical challenge and more a test of organizational readiness. Managing hype, fostering a culture of innovation, and establishing robust governance are the most critical determinants of success.
