Prioritizing the First Move

The selection of initial pilot projects is a critical strategic decision. The goal is to find "Quick Wins" that deliver tangible value, build momentum, and serve as powerful internal success stories. Explore the matrix to understand how to classify potential projects.

Quick Wins (Prioritize Now)

High Impact, High Feasibility, Low Effort. The ideal starting point. Delivers measurable value quickly, builds momentum, and minimizes risk.

Strategic Bets

High Impact, Low Feasibility/High Effort. Transformative potential but requires significant investment. Plan for the future, not for initial pilots.

Incremental Gains

Low Impact, High Feasibility, Low Effort. Easy to implement but won't move the needle strategically. Useful for training or small-scale experimentation.

Avoid for Now

Low Impact, Low Feasibility/High Effort. High cost for little return. These projects drain resources and morale.
