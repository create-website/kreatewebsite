The Autonomy Dilemma

Agentic AI, systems that can act independently to achieve goals, are no longer science fiction. They promise to revolutionize industries, but this leap in autonomy brings forth a complex array of technical, ethical, and legal challenges that we must navigate with caution and foresight.

The Black Box Problem

The decision-making processes of complex AI models can be opaque. This lack of transparency makes it difficult to trust, debug, or verify their actions are fair and logical.

Navigating Legal Gray Areas

Existing legal frameworks were created for human actors. Agentic AI challenges core legal concepts from ownership to liability, creating a need for new laws and regulations. The central issue is the question of "Legal Agency," which has a direct impact on all other areas of law.
